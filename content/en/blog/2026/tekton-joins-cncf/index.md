We're thrilled to announce that the [CNCF Technical Oversight Committee
(TOC)](https://www.cncf.io/people/technical-oversight-committee/) has voted to
accept Tekton as a [CNCF](https://www.cncf.io/) incubating project!

This is a landmark moment for the Tekton community and marks the beginning of an
exciting new chapter for the project.

Read the full announcements:

- **CNCF**: [Tekton Becomes a CNCF Incubating
  Project](https://www.cncf.io/blog/2026/03/24/tekton-becomes-a-cncf-incubating-project/)
- **CD Foundation**: [Tekton Moves to the
  CNCF](https://cd.foundation/announcement/2026/03/24/tekton-moves-to-the-cncf/)

## From the CDF to the CNCF

Tekton was [donated to the Continuous Delivery Foundation
(CDF)](https://cd.foundation/blog/2019/03/12/introducing-the-continuous-delivery-foundation-the-new-home-for-tekton-jenkins-jenkins-x-and-spinnaker/)
in March 2019, shortly after its creation. The CDF provided a nurturing home
where Tekton grew from an early-stage project into a
[graduated](https://tekton.dev/blog/2022/10/26/tekton-graduation/) open source
project with a vibrant community and broad adoption across the industry.

We're deeply grateful to the CDF and the Linux Foundation for their years of
support, mentorship, and stewardship. The foundation played a critical role in
Tekton's growth — from establishing governance and release processes to
sponsoring an [independent security
audit](https://cd.foundation/blog/2022/08/26/tekton-security-review-completed/)
and helping the project reach graduation.

Moving to the CNCF places Tekton alongside other foundational cloud-native
projects like Kubernetes, Knative, and Argo — projects that Tekton already
integrates with and complements in production environments around the world.

## What This Means for Users and Contributors

For existing Tekton users: **nothing changes today**. Tekton's APIs, releases,
and support policies remain the same. You can continue using Tekton exactly as
you do now.

For contributors: the project will transition its governance and infrastructure
to align with CNCF processes over time. The community will communicate any
changes as they happen. Contribution workflows, communication channels, and the
welcoming culture of the project remain the same.

## What's Next

The Tekton community will work through the transition over the coming months.
We'll share updates on any changes to infrastructure, governance, or processes
as they happen.

In the meantime, we continue to ship — with recent releases like [Tekton
Pipelines v1.10.0](https://tekton.dev/blog/2026/02/12/tekton-pipelines-v1.10.0/)
and [Pipelines-as-Code joining the Tekton
organization](https://tekton.dev/blog/2026/03/19/pipelines-as-code-joins-the-tekton-organization/),
the project has never been more active.

Thank you to everyone who has contributed to Tekton over the years — whether
through code, documentation, issue reports, talks, blog posts, or simply using
the project and sharing feedback. This milestone belongs to the entire community.

Welcome to the CNCF, Tekton! 🎉
